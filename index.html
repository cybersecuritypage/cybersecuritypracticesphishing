Types of Phishing Attacks

Phishing attacks use different tactics to trick people into sharing sensitive information or taking unsafe actions. Knowing the common types helps you recognize threats quickly.

Common Types of Phishing:

  • Double‑Barreled Phishing

    A combined attack using more than one method, such as an email followed by a phone call or text, to increase pressure and credibility.

  • Spear Phishing

    A targeted attack aimed at a specific person or team, often using personal or work‑related details to appear legitimate.

  • Smishing

    Phishing carried out through text messages, often impersonating delivery services, banks, or IT support.

  • Vishing

    Voice‑based phishing where attackers call while pretending to be trusted contacts such as managers or IT staff.
